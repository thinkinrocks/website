Bambu Lab P1S is an enclosed CoreXY 3D printer optimized for fast, repeatable printing of prototypes and functional parts. The enclosure improves reliability for temperature-sensitive filaments and reduces warping for larger parts.

The AMS 2 Pro enables automatic filament switching for multi-material and multi-color prints, supports faster iteration for mechanical assemblies, and simplifies workflows like printing soluble supports or multi-part prototypes in one job.

Typical use cases include robotics brackets, sensor mounts, custom enclosures, quick mechanical iterations, and fixtures/jigs for lab setups.

**Development & Lab Advice:** For functional parts, validate tolerances with quick calibration prints (holes, press-fits) before long jobs. Prefer PETG/ABS/ASA for durable prototypes; plan ventilation when printing higher-temperature materials.
